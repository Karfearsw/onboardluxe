The Ocean Luxe Workflow

This lesson orients you to how Ocean Luxe handles leads end-to-end and what "good" looks like in your first week.

  1. Capture — A lead comes in (web form, referral, list, inbound call, etc.). Someone records it in the system immediately so nothing falls through the cracks.
  2. Contact — We reach out quickly, professionally, and compliantly. Speed matters: leads that are contacted within minutes convert at a much higher rate than those that wait hours.
  3. Qualify / Advance — We determine fit and set a clear next step — an appointment, a follow-up date, or a close-out. Never end a conversation without knowing what happens next.
Key Principle

Your job is not just to "make calls." It's to move every lead to a clear next step — or close it out cleanly so no one wastes time on a dead-end.

Professional Standards

  • Be clear, respectful, and direct. No pressure language, no exaggeration — Ocean Luxe's reputation travels with every call you make.
  • Confirm understanding before you hang up. Always agree on what happens next (date, time, next action) before ending any interaction.
  • Log what happened immediately. Notes in the CRM let the next teammate pick up exactly where you left off — clean handoffs are non-negotiable.

Compliance & Ethics

Note

This is not legal advice. Always follow Ocean Luxe policy and local regulations.

  • Be truthful about who you are and why you're calling. Never misrepresent your role, your company, or what you're offering.
  • Honor opt-outs immediately. If someone asks not to be contacted again, log it, stop outreach, and never call them back without express re-consent.
  • Protect data. Only share or store customer information in Ocean Luxe-approved systems (the CRM). No screenshots to personal phones, no sharing outside the team.
